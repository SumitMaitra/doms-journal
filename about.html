About the Journal

NITI: Journal of Management Research strives to encourage and disseminate high-quality research on the ways in which business, institutional and public policies shape management practice, governance and societal outcomes.

Our Mission

The journal emphasizes the dynamic relationship between institutional performance and policy design, particularly in the context of emerging economies where policy decisions have profound implications for sustainable development.

By fostering dialogue across disciplines and sectors, NITI provides a platform for scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to critically examine how policies influence institutions and markets. In doing so, the journal bridges theory and practice, offering insights that not only advance academic knowledge but also guide effective and sustainable systems of governance in real-world decision-making.

Focus Areas

Business Policies

Research on how business policies influence organizational strategy, governance, and market dynamics.

Institutional Policies

Examination of institutional frameworks and their impact on management practices and performance.

Public Policies

Analysis of public policy design and implementation in management and governance contexts.

Emerging Economies

Special emphasis on policy challenges and opportunities in emerging economy contexts.

Department of Management Studies, NIT Durgapur

NITI: Journal of Management Research is affiliated with the Department of Management Studies (DoMS), National Institute of Technology Durgapur. DoMS has established itself as a leading center for management education and research in India.

The department's research portfolio spans critical areas including Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, Systems, and Operations. With a strong emphasis on sustainability, digital transformation, and data-driven decision-making, DoMS fosters both interdisciplinary and industry-oriented research approaches.

This commitment to academic rigor, practical relevance, and global engagement makes DoMS an ideal institutional home for NITI's mission to bridge theory and practice in management and policy research.

Management Doctoral Research Colloquium (MDRC)

The Management Doctoral Research Colloquium (MDRC), organized by the Department of Management Studies, NIT Durgapur, is a flagship platform committed to advancing scholarly dialogue in the field of management research.

MDRC brings together doctoral scholars, early-career academics, and researchers from diverse domains to present their research, receive constructive feedback, and foster research collaborations. The colloquium emphasizes academic rigor, interdisciplinary thinking, and mentorship-driven engagement.

International Conference

The annual International Conference serves as a confluence of renowned faculties, distinguished academicians, leading industry experts, researchers, and visionary leaders from across the country and the world.

The conference aims to deliberate on contemporary issues and frontier innovations in business, sustainability, leadership, technology, and policy—themes that align closely with NITI's editorial focus.

Partnership with SAGE Publishing

NITI: Journal of Management Research fits strategically within the SAGE journals program by expanding its global, interdisciplinary, and policy-focused portfolio.

While SAGE already publishes strong titles in management and governance, few journals integrate policy, strategy, and management with a focus on emerging economies. NITI fills this gap by:

Aligning with SAGE's global strategy to grow research from the Global South and diversify perspectives

Complementing existing journals through an interdisciplinary focus

Expanding SAGE's reach to policymakers, business leaders, and academics in emerging markets

Offering portfolio differentiation with emphasis on cross-sectoral insights and practical policy impact

This positioning strengthens SAGE's presence in management and policy research while opening new geographic and thematic frontiers, supporting SAGE's mission of advancing impactful, globally relevant research.

Bridging Theory and Practice

NITI is committed to generating actionable insights that serve academics, practitioners, and policymakers alike. We believe that effective governance and sustainable development require both rigorous scholarship and practical wisdom drawn from real-world experience.
