Rationale of the Journal

Why NITI is Needed

The rationale behind NITI: Journal of Management Research lies in the growing need to understand how policies—whether public, business, or organizational—shape the functioning of institutions, markets, and societies in an increasingly complex and interconnected world.

While much of the policy discourse has traditionally been framed from the perspectives of developed economies, the realities of emerging economies demand greater scholarly and practice-oriented attention.

The Challenge

Emerging economies are marked by unique governance challenges, resource constraints, and opportunities for innovation that call for fresh insights into policy design, implementation, and impact.

The Solution

NITI seeks to address this gap by providing a dedicated platform for research that examines the role of policies in management and governance from multidisciplinary and cross-sectoral perspectives.

Bridging Theory and Practice

By emphasizing pluralism in approaches and methodologies, the journal aspires to bridge the divide between theory and practice, enabling a richer understanding of how policies can foster:

Effective Leadership
Sustainable Growth
Institutional Resilience
Ethical Governance

Contributing to Knowledge and Practice

In doing so, NITI contributes to advancing both academic knowledge and practical solutions that support policymakers, business leaders, and managers in shaping organizations and societies that are better equipped to meet contemporary global and local challenges.

For Scholars

Advancing theoretical and empirical knowledge in policy and management.

For Practitioners

Providing actionable insights for effective policy implementation.

For Policymakers

Supporting evidence-based decision-making for sustainable development.
