Target Audience and Market Potential

NITI: Journal of Management Research is designed for a broad readership that includes academics, researchers, policymakers, business leaders, managers, and practitioners engaged in the study and practice of management, governance, and policy.

Our Diverse Readership

Scholars and Researchers

Academics in management, public policy, organizational studies, economics, political science, and related disciplines who seek to advance theoretical and empirical knowledge on the role of policies in shaping organizations and societies.

Policymakers and Government Officials

Professionals involved in designing, implementing, and evaluating public and business policies, especially within the context of emerging economies.

Business Leaders and Managers

Executives and managers interested in understanding how policy environments affect organizational strategy, governance practices, and market dynamics.

Students and Early-Career Researchers

Graduate students and emerging scholars looking for critical insights into the intersections of management, governance, and policy in contemporary contexts.

Practitioners and Consultants

Professionals engaged in policy design, organizational development, and governance reform who wish to ground their work in evidence-based research.

Fostering Cross-Sector Dialogue

By engaging this diverse audience, NITI aims to foster dialogue across academia, government, and industry, encouraging the exchange of ideas that inform effective, ethical, and sustainable systems of management and governance.
