About the Journal

NITI: Journal of Management Research strives to encourage and disseminate high-quality research on the ways in which business, institutional and public policies shape management practice, governance and societal outcomes.

The journal emphasizes the dynamic relationship between institutional performance and policy design, particularly in the context of emerging economies where policy decisions have profound implications for sustainable development.

By fostering dialogue across disciplines and sectors, NITI provides a platform for scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to critically examine how policies influence institutions and markets. In doing so, the journal bridges theory and practice, offering insights that not only advance academic knowledge but also guide effective and sustainable systems of governance in real-world decision-making.

Aims and Scope

NITI: Journal of Management Research is a peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing both scholarly and practice-oriented understanding of business, institutional and public policies in the context of management and governance. The journal provides a platform for critical inquiry into the role of policies in shaping organizations, markets, and societies, with a particular emphasis on the realities, challenges, and opportunities of emerging economies.

The journal adopts a pluralistic approach to policymaking, encouraging contributions that embrace multiple perspectives, diverse methodologies, and interdisciplinary engagement. NITI welcomes research that examines policies across multiple levels—from organizational and sectoral contexts to national and global governance systems—highlighting their impact on management practices, institutional performance, and socio-economic development.

In seeking to bridge theory and practice, NITI invites original research, conceptual papers, case studies, and policy analyses that generate actionable insights for leaders, managers, and policymakers. Contributions may address a wide range of themes, including but not limited to sustainable governance, ethical leadership, innovation in policy design and implementation, policy impacts on entrepreneurship and markets, and the interplay between local contexts and global policy trends.

Rationale of the Journal

The rationale behind NITI: Journal of Management Research lies in the growing need to understand how policies—whether public, business, or organizational—shape the functioning of institutions, markets, and societies in an increasingly complex and interconnected world. While much of the policy discourse has traditionally been framed from the perspectives of developed economies, the realities of emerging economies demand greater scholarly and practice-oriented attention.

These contexts are marked by unique governance challenges, resource constraints, and opportunities for innovation that call for fresh insights into policy design, implementation, and impact. NITI seeks to address this gap by providing a dedicated platform for research that examines the role of policies in management and governance from multidisciplinary and cross-sectoral perspectives.

Bridging Theory & Practice

By emphasizing pluralism in approaches and methodologies, the journal aspires to bridge the divide between theory and practice, enabling a richer understanding of how policies can foster effective leadership, sustainable growth, institutional resilience, and ethical governance. In doing so, NITI contributes to advancing both academic knowledge and practical solutions that support policymakers, business leaders, and managers.
