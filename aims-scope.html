Aims and Scope of the Journal

NITI: Journal of Management Research is a peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing both scholarly and practice-oriented understanding of business, institutional and public policies in the context of management and governance.

Platform for Critical Inquiry

The journal provides a platform for critical inquiry into the role of policies in shaping organizations, markets, and societies, with a particular emphasis on the realities, challenges, and opportunities of emerging economies.

Pluralistic Approach

The journal adopts a pluralistic approach to policymaking, encouraging contributions that embrace multiple perspectives, diverse methodologies, and interdisciplinary engagement. NITI welcomes research that examines policies across multiple levels—from organizational and sectoral contexts to national and global governance systems—highlighting their impact on management practices, institutional performance, and socio-economic development.

Types of Contributions

In seeking to bridge theory and practice, NITI invites original research, conceptual papers, case studies, and policy analyses that generate actionable insights for leaders, managers, and policymakers.

Original Research

Empirical studies and theoretical contributions advancing the field.

Conceptual Papers

Theoretical frameworks and conceptual models.

Case Studies

Real-world applications and contextual analyses.

Policy Analyses

Critical examination of policy design and implementation.

Research Themes

Contributions may address a wide range of themes, including but not limited to:

Sustainable Governance

Policies and practices for long-term organizational and societal sustainability.

Ethical Leadership

Leadership practices grounded in ethical principles and values.

Innovation in Policy Design

Novel approaches to policy formulation and implementation.

Entrepreneurship and Markets

Policy impacts on entrepreneurial ecosystems and market dynamics.

Local and Global Policy Trends

The interplay between local contexts and global governance frameworks.

Institutional Performance

Analysis of how policies affect organizational effectiveness and efficiency.

Multidisciplinary Engagement

By fostering dialogue across disciplines and sectors, NITI aims to deepen understanding of how policies can drive effective, ethical, and sustainable systems of governance—contributing to knowledge creation and informed decision-making in business, government, and society.
