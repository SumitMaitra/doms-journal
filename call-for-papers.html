Call for Papers

We Welcome Your Submissions!

NITI: Journal of Management Research invites scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to submit their research for publication.

General Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original research articles, conceptual papers, case studies, and policy analyses that contribute to the understanding of how policies shape management practices, governance, and societal outcomes.

Topics of Interest Include (But Are Not Limited To):

Sustainable Governance

Policies for long-term organizational and societal sustainability

Ethical Leadership

Leadership practices grounded in ethical principles

Innovation in Policy Design

Novel approaches to policy formulation and implementation

Entrepreneurship and Markets

Policy impacts on entrepreneurial ecosystems

Institutional Performance

Analysis of policy effects on organizational effectiveness

Global and Local Policy Trends

Interplay between local contexts and global frameworks

Emerging Economy Contexts

Unique governance challenges and opportunities

Policy Evaluation

Assessment of policy outcomes and impacts

Important Dates

Rolling Submissions: We accept submissions on a rolling basis. Specific deadlines for special issues will be announced separately.

Submission Requirements

  • Manuscripts must be original and not under consideration elsewhere
  • Follow the submission guidelines
  • Prepare manuscript according to the specified format
  • Include abstract (150-250 words) and 4-6 keywords
  • Provide author information and affiliations
  • Declare any conflicts of interest
